About Us

About Us

The Jersey Shore is famous for many things, beautiful beaches, nonstop nightlife, and family fun. Another thing the Jersey Shore does like no other is their fabulous dining options. Jimbo's, a Seaside Heights bar and grille, has the perfect mix of summer fun, family friendly, and never ending nights. Not only does the menu boast more variety than anything you would typically come across in a boardwalk setting, but its also prepared with all the freshest ingredients. Cheese steaks, panini's, pizza, and butterfly fries are just the beginning. We also special in succulent ribs, satisfying salads, and an array of decadent dessert like fried oreos. Children love the fun dining area and are encouraged to take pictures with the many famous statues that are scattered around the 50's themed building. The action doesn't stop after you eat either. Jimbo's has the largest selection of spirits on the strip. Our knowledgeable bartenders will make your old favorites or familiarize you with many of our own specialty drinks. Entertainment varies from Sir Cedrick, our steel drum player, to crowd pleasing bands, to the dj's that the Jersey Shore is so famous for. Next time your in the area, check out Jimbo's Bar and Grill in Seaside Heights, NJ!
